This week's news reveals an AI industry simultaneously expanding and consolidating. Yet this concentration is enabling broader access: DeepSeek's trillion-parameter open-source release, Perplexity's system-level AI, healthcare tools reaching 30,000+ NHS workers.

For practitioners, the message is clear: the foundation model layer is consolidating around players with billion-dollar compute budgets, but the application layer—where AI meets specific industries, workflows, and problems—remains wide open. The companies thriving in this funding environment aren't building yet another model; they're building the infrastructure, tooling, and vertical solutions that make models useful.

The next six months will test whether this capital deployment translates into proportional capability gains—or whether we're witnessing the most expensive plateau in technology history.
