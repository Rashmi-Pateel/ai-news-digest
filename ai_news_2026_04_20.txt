### 2. 🤝 Novo Nordisk Partners with OpenAI for Drug Discovery

**Why it matters:** Finding new drugs usually takes 10+ years and billions of dollars. AI could slash that time dramatically, meaning life-saving medicines reach patients faster.

### 4. 🛡️ NVIDIA Launches "Ising" - AI Models for Quantum Computers

**Why it matters:** Quantum computers are supposed to be the next big thing in tech, but they're still hard to use. This tool could be the bridge that makes them practical for real problems.

### 5. 🔍 Universal Deepfake Detector Achieves Record Accuracy
Scientists created a new AI tool that can spot fake AI-generated videos better than ever before. It works across different types of deepfakes, making it harder for bad actors to spread misinformation.

**Why it matters:** Deepfakes are getting scary good at fooling people. Having a reliable "lie detector" for videos helps protect us from scams, political manipulation, and fake news.
